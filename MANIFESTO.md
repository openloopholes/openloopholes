# The OpenLoopholes Manifesto

We are taxed to death.

We pay taxes on the food we put on our tables, the income we earn with our hands, the homes we bust our asses to own, every mile we drive to work, every investment we try to grow, and — in the final insult — even on what we leave behind when we die.

And what do we get for it?

**Endless wars.**  
**Rampant waste.**  
**Fraud and abuse** so brazen it should make every honest American furious.

We elect politicians with hope — they promise real change.  
Instead, they walk into the halls of Congress and become the problem: lining their own pockets and those of their friends while the rest of us get squeezed harder every year.

Meanwhile, billionaires hire armies of lobbyists and tax lawyers to carve out custom loopholes the rest of us will never see.  
The rich keep getting richer.  
The middle class is vanishing.

The tax code itself — now over half a million pages of special-interest corruption — has become a weapon of oppression aimed squarely at working people.

This is **not** the America our Founders envisioned.

A Constitution inspired by God-given liberty and limited government built the greatest engine of prosperity the world has ever known.  
Yet today those same halls have been turned into a **den of thieves**.  
Government overreach and unaccountability have reached a fever pitch.

**Enough.**

It’s time to stop feeding the beast and start starving it — **legally**.

**OpenLoopholes** is a rebellion.

We are weaponizing open-source AI to do what the elites have done for decades: hunt down every single intended and unintended **legal** loophole in that bloated tax code and hand the power back to everyday Americans.

Our goal is bold and unapologetic:  
**Help one million regular Americans legally drive their tax liability as close to zero as humanly possible** — so they can keep every hard-earned penny in their own pocket instead of handing it over to the very politicians who have failed them.

This isn’t tax evasion.  
**This is tax intelligence.**  
This is using the system against itself.
