§ 139J. Certain contributions to Trump accounts(a) In generalGross income of an account beneficiary shall not include any qualified general contribution to a Trump account of the account beneficiary.


(b) DefinitionsAny term used in this section which is used in section 530A shall have the meaning given such term under section 530A.


(Added Pub. L. 119–21, title VII, § 70204(c)(1), July 4, 2025, 139 Stat. 186.)
